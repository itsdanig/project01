About Us

Worldwide Yoga Wellness Retreats

Our organization was originally founded in 2002, Ontario, Canada when a group of us instructors were having a yoga session. We knew combining nature, culture an yoga would refresh the mind and body and could not resist to start our now very successful organization. We have many retreat destinations to match what you''re looking for all year round. ' Some of our retreat destinations include Dubai, Egypt, Chile and Peru! Our team makes sure each individual has the best experience they could possibly achieve and will be there to teach and guide you along the way.

Dubai

One of our top destinations and definitely one of the staff favourites. Dubai is full of culture, beauty and good food.

Chile

Chile has incredible views which can't hurt while finding ourselves & practicing yoga. Chile has some of the best comfort food our team has ever had. Their culture is unlike any other and the people are incredible. This hidden jewel is definitely one we always recommend!

Egypt

This location is breathtaking and super adventurous! Practicing yoga in Egypt is incredibly life changing. join us on a retreat to Egypt!

Peru

Another staff favourite, Peru! Peru is an incredible country, with incredible food and people. Their scenery is nothing like we see in North America! Come hang out in Peru!
